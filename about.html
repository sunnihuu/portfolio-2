Hi, I'm Sunni (Sunny)

I’m a designer exploring the intersection of brand and space.

Trained as a graphic designer at Pratt Institute, I built a strong foundation in identity systems and visual communication. I have collaborated with cultural institutions and design studios including BAM, UCCA Center for Contemporary Art, Meanwhile Partners, The Metropolitan Museum of Art, Junzi, Nice Day Chinese, and the Center for Book Arts.

Currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computational Design at Columbia University GSAPP, I translate brand systems into spatial experiences grounded in clarity, consistency, and human experience.

Outside of work, you’ll find me knitting, watching films, catching a baseball game, or playing tennis.
