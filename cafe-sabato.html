Café Sabato

Slow coffee + inspired cocktails

ClientCafé Sabato (Independent café concept)

LocationNew York, NY

Year2024

RoleBrand + Graphic Designer

ScopeBrand Strategy, Visual Identity System, Guidelines, Packaging, Menu + Print, Environmental Graphics Concepts, Spatial Branding Direction

Cafe Sabato is an independent New York coffee concept rooted in slow living, thoughtful design, and community gathering. I developed a visual identity and brand system that balances hand-drawn warmth with intentional structure, extending across packaging, menus, and spatial touchpoints. The result is a cohesive experience that feels both personal and crafted.
