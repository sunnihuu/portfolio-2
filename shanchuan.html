ShanChuan Wine

Artisanal wine meets modern craftsmanship

ClientShanChuan Wine

LocationBeijing, China

Year2024

RoleBrand Designer

ScopeBrand Identity, Wine Label Design, Packaging System, Merchandise Design, Menu + Collateral

ShanChuan Wine is a boutique winery that bridges traditional Chinese wine culture with contemporary design sensibilities. The brand identity embodies the harmonious balance between heritage and innovation, reflecting the craftsmanship behind every bottle.

The visual system extends across multiple touchpoints — from wine labels and packaging to merchandise and tasting room materials — creating a cohesive brand experience that celebrates both the terroir and the artistry of winemaking.
